About Tekken

Tekken is a long-running and beloved fighting game series, known for its diverse roster, unique gameplay mechanics, and competitive nature. The latest installment in the series, Tekken 8, offers stunning graphics, intense combat, and rich storylines for each character.

Featured Characters

  • Kazuya Mishima
  • Jin Kazama
  • Heihachi Mishima
  • King
  • Nina Williams

Gameplay Features

Tekken offers a robust fighting system with a mix of traditional and new mechanics. The Rage system, combo chains, and special moves are pivotal in delivering an immersive fighting experience.

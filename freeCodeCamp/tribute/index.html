Tribute to Heath Ledger

Heath Ledger was an Australian actor who captivated audiences with his remarkable performances. He is best known for his role as the Joker in *The Dark Knight*, for which he posthumously won an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor. His portrayal of the character was iconic, showcasing his incredible talent and dedication to his craft.

Ledger's career included a variety of roles in films like *Brokeback Mountain*, *10 Things I Hate About You*, and *A Knight's Tale*. He is remembered not only for his impressive filmography but also for his charisma and passion for acting.
